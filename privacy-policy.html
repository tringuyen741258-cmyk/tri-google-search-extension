Privacy Policy

Tri Google Search Extension does not collect, store, use, or share any personal data from users.

What information we collect

This extension does not collect or store any identifying information, browsing history, cookies, or search queries.

How your data is used

Your search keywords are used locally on your device only to open a new Google Search tab. No data is transmitted to any external server.

Permissions explanation

The extension uses browser permissions strictly to open a new Google Search tab and improve user convenience. These permissions do not grant access to personal data.
